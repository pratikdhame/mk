About Us

The Gayatri Grapes Garden Story

Founded in 2010, Gayatri Grapes Garden has been dedicated to growing the finest quality grapes and watermelons in the fertile soils of Maharashtra. Our commitment to sustainable farming practices ensures that every fruit we produce is not only delicious but also grown with respect for nature.

We take pride in our expert farming techniques passed down through generations, combined with modern agricultural innovations to deliver fruits that exceed industry standards.

At Gayatri Grapes Garden, we believe in transparent farm-to-table practices, allowing our customers to buy with confidence and our farmers to sell with pride.
